Course Description

This course is designed to provide the students with a thorough understanding of the importance of money and banking in various economies. It aims to provide the students with an introduction to understand the concept of money, theories of money supply and money demand and working of monetary policy.

Course Outcome

On successful completion of this program, students would be able to:

  • Understand the various services offered and various risks faced by banks
  • Understand the dynamic changes of the banking industry and the policy responses because of the recent crisis
  • Have a practical understanding of the various distribution channels of insurance products for effective marketing.
  • Have a practical understanding of regulations and be able to understand insurance operations both in life as well as general insurance.
